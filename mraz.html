Mráz (2017)

Composer: Trevor Bača.
Forces: piano.
Duration: 5 minutes.

Final page of Mráz (2017) for piano

Program note

The following inscription, written by the composer, appears at the head of the score:

Under stone, under feather, around the edges of fruit bristles ice in its tracings until morning. So small but so bright it is the crystals in night that give to the frost its serrations. What tracks are these that back-tread to dreams in grains that arise and enjamb?.

First performances

World premiere given by Jared Redmond on 19 April 2017 in Hertz Hall on the campus of the University of California, Berkeley.

US East Coast premiere given by Jared Redmond on 5 May 2017 in Slosberg Recital Studio on the campus of Brendeis University in Waltham, Massachusetts.

Asian premiere given by Jared Redmond on 30 May 2018 in Paiknam Concert Hall on the campus of Hanyang University in Seoul, South Korea.
