The music of composer Trevor Bača

( H A R M O N Y ) (2019)

Composer: Trevor Bača.
Forces: narrator & nine players.
Duration: 20 minutes.

First performances

World premiere by Jonathan Hepfer and Echoi Ensemble on 13 January 2020 at Zipper Hall as part of Monday Evening Concerts in Los Angeles, California.

( H A R M O N Y ) was commissioned by the Monday Evening Concerts and was made possible by the Chamber Music America Classical Commissioning Program, with generous funding provided by The Andrew W. Mellon Foundation, and the Chamber Music America Endowment Fund.
